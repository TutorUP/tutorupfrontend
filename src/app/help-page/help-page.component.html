How to Use Tutor UP

Seeking to learn

  • View the the list of tutors (link to tutor page)
  • Filtering by major or course will narrow down the list to help you find tutors for specific needs.
  • Feel free to explore the profiles!
  • Reach out to tutors that seem promising, describe your need clearly so they know what kind of help you are looking for!

Seeking to teach

  • Provide a fun bio info about yourself (if you want to).
  • It would be helpful to everyone if you gave your available hours.
  • Be sure to list the subjects/skills that you are most comfortable with.
  • Avoid listing subjects that you don't know well enough to teach to someone else.
  • Be sure to check for messages from students looking for help!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

In order to sign in to the application, use your UP username and password. Login here!

How do I contact a tutor?

To contact a tutor, click on their profile page. There you will find their email address! Make sure to use a strong subject line so they know what you're emailing about!

How do I leave a review?

To leave a review, search for the user and click 'Leave a Review'.

How do I report an innappropriate review?

To report a review, email tutorupadmin@up.edu.

How do I find UP hired tutors?

To find a UP hired tutor, use the filters on the home page!

How do I know I'll be paid?

Payment transactions are handled by the tutor and the tutee. Ask for payment at the beginning of the session if you are worried.

What do I do if someone outside of UP contacts me?

If someone outside of UP contacts you, report it to tutorupadmin@up.edu immediately.
